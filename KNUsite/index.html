Innovative Waste Management for a Sustainable Future

About Our Project

KNU aims to tackle waste management issues by recycling hair collected from barbershops and salons. This hair can be used in various industries, such as oil absorption, composting, and textiles. Our goal is to turn waste into a valuable resource.

Our Mission

To reduce environmental waste and promote sustainability through innovative recycling methods.
